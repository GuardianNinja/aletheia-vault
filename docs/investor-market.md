# Investor Market

The Investor Market enables individuals and institutions to fund inventions
and participate in future royalty streams.

---

## Core concepts

- **Invention Investment Listings**
  - Inventors may list inventions as open for investment.
  - Listings include technical summaries, risk notes, and funding goals.

- **Revenue-Share Positions**
  - Investors can purchase rights to a share of future royalties.
  - Positions are recorded as digital contracts in the core database.
  - Optional tokenization can mirror positions on-chain.

- **Investor Protections**
  - Standardized disclosures required for listings.
  - Risk scoring and due diligence summaries.
  - Escrow for funds until defined milestones are hit.

---

## AI assistance

- Summaries of:
  - Prior art context.
  - Market potential signals.
  - Technical feasibility factors.

These are advisory only and do not constitute investment advice.

---

## Integration with the JARVONDIS Reserve

- Weekly, 2% of the **gross transaction volume** in the Investor Market
  is routed into the JARVONDIS Reserve.

- This contribution:
  - Strengthens payout stability for inventors.
  - Helps protect the system during downturns.
